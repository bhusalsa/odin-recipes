Make delicious Chicken Katsu in 30 minutes!


Katsu is a Japanese dish of crispy fried cutlets coated with Panko bread crumbs. Popular varieties are chicken katsu (like this recipe) and tonkatsu (which is made with pork).

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • All-purpose flour
  • Egg
  • Panko(Bread crumbs)
  • Oil

How to make the Chicken Katsu?

  1. Prepare and cut the chicken breast into halves.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge the seasoned chicken in flour. It will help to seal the moisture.
  4. Coat each breast in egg and then press into the Panko(Bread crumbs).
  5. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
    6. Your Chicken Katsu is ready. It can be served alongside Kombu Seasweed Salad, Miso Soup or anything else you like.
