Make Special Chicken Momo at home

Momo is a popular Tibetan and Nepali dumpling, typically steamed and filled with meat or vegetables. This chicken version is flavorful, juicy, and perfect for sharing!.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken mince
  • Onion
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Corriander
  • Salt
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Oil

How to make the Chicken Momo?

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt and water. Knead until smooth and let it rest.
  2. Finely chop onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and cilantro.
  3. Mix the chopped ingredients with chicken mince, a little oil, salt, and soy sauce to make the filling.
  4. Roll the dough into thin circles (about 3 inches in diameter).
  5. Place a spoonful of the chicken filling in the center of each wrapper.
  6. Fold and pinch the edges to seal the momos into a desired shape.
  7. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 12-15 minutes or until the dough is cooked and the filling is juicy.
    8. Your Chicken Momos are ready! Serve them hot with spicy tomato chutney or soy sauce.
