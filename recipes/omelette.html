his easy, fail-proof omelette recipe is truly the best.

With a variety of filling suggestions, you will learn how to make an omelet at home that's even more delicious than your favorite breakfast restaurant

Ingredients:

  • ½ Tablespoon salted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • Pinch salt and pepper
  • ¼ cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegetables, diced small (bell pepper, zucchini, onion, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 Tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 slices bacon, diced

How to make the Best Omelette Recipe?

  1. Dice 1 cup of vegetables.
  2. Over medium-high heat, sauté the vegetables and garlic in olive oil until soft, about 3-5 minutes depending on the vegetables you choose.
  3. Set aside.
  4. Cook bacon (or sausage), garlic, and onion over medium heat until bacon is cooked and onion is soft (5-8 minutes).
  5. Set aside.
  6. Whisk eggs vigorously until they are homogenous (uniform throughout).
  7. Whisk in salt and pepper.
  8. Melt butter in an 8” nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
  9. Add whisked eggs and swirl the pan until they are evenly distributed.
  10. Cover the pan and cook over medium-low heat until the eggs begin to set.
  11. Once eggs begin to set, gently push the edges in with a spatula and swirl the pan so uncooked eggs on top move to the edges.
  12. When the omelette is mostly set (about 5-6 minutes), carefully flip it and turn off the heat.
  13. Sprinkle cheese over the top of the omelette.
  14. Add the fillings on one half of the omelette.
  15. Fold the other half over the fillings.
  16. Serve immediately.
