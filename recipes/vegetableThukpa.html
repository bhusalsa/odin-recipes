Make Hot Vegetable Thukpa at home

Vegetable thukpa is a favorite dish during the Tibetan New Year celebration in Nepal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 green chile peppers, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped lemongrass
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 ounces sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ajwain
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (6 ounce) package fresh spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

How to make the Chicken Momo?

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil.
  2. Add egg noodles and boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 7 to 9 minutes.
  3. Drain, rinse, and drain again. Set aside.
  4. While the egg noodles cook, heat mustard oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  5. Add onion and fry until light brown, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add chiles, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, cumin, and turmeric.
  7. Sauté the mixture for 1 minute.
  8. Add bell pepper and mushrooms.
  9. Stir-fry for 6 minutes.
  10. Add broth, tomatoes, yogurt, soy sauce, ajwain, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  11. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  12. Add egg noodles and simmer for 3 minutes.
  13. Stir in spinach and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  14. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
    15. Your Vegetable Thukpa is ready! Serve them hot and enjoy with your family.
